Matthew Fricke


George Matthew Fricke received his M.S. in computer science at the University of New Mexico. He now owns Go Figure Software which has contracted with the Theoretical Biology Team at Los Alamos National Labs to produce RuleBuilder, a graphical interface to the BioNetGen software. His work has focused on computer solutions to problems in molecular biology including the modeling of inter-cellular protein interactions during signaling and the use of competitive neural networks in the reconstruction of phylogenic trees.
