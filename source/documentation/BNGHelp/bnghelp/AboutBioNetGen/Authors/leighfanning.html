Leigh Fanning


Leigh Fanning received a B.S. in Engineering Physics from the University of Colorado at Boulder. She is currently the owner of Versiera, a privately held firm specializing in software engineering, computer security and computer systems technology. She has over ten years professional experience in modeling, simulation and testing in a wide variety of applications including Space Shuttle guidance, navigation and control, DOE Hanford Site tank safety management and NOAA sponsored ozone monitoring.
